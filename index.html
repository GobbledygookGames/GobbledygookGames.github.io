Antiventure

Antiventure is a single-player turn-based strategy / RPG game where you must protect your town from a plundering plague of adventurers. An enchantment has been placed upon the land, preventing any form of violence and bodily harm. So if you can't beat them, how will you deal with this infestation of kleptomaniacs? The villagers will have to resort to blocking, barring, distracting, fooling, taunting, entertaining and bamboozling the adventurers while nailing down everything they own that isn't already nailed down. Use your remaining resources to build up and resupply your town so the villagers have a chance to brave the next wave of "heroes".
