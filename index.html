Antiventure

Antiventure is a single-player turn-based strategy / RPG game where you must protect your town from a plundering plague of adventurers. Since you can't beat them, how will you deal with this infestation of kleptomaniacs? Your villagers will have to resort to blocking, barring, distracting, fooling, taunting, entertaining and bamboozling the adventurers while nailing down everything they own that isn't nailed down already. The town will have to make do with what remains after the latest mob of murderhobos has moved on to their next place to plunder. Rebuild and resupply your town so the villagers have a chance to brave the next wave of "heroes".

They will kick down your door and always want more! So tell me, how will you outwit your next gaggle of adventurers?.
